Antoine Berthelot

Côte des Bar

One of Champagne's most exciting new voices. Launched in 2022 on steep, south-facing family parcels in the Côte des Bar, Antoine Berthelot brings rare precision to micro-production winemaking. Domaine Pierre & Adrien Berlancourt

Meursault

A family micro-estate of just 1.6ha (organic) created in Meursault in 2005, working exclusively with Bourgogne appellation plots — both whites and reds border Puligny-Montrachet — yet treated as if they were Grand Crus, with meticulous handwork and long, careful ageing in thoughtfully selected barrels. All wines are aged at least two years: whites undergo 18–24 months of oak ageing (30% to 100% new) on fine lees, Coche-Dury style, while the reds are made entirely with whole clusters (3-week maceration) and aged 24 months. All wines bottled manually, without filtration. Pierre and Adrien farm at very low yields — around 25 hl/ha versus an area average of 65 hl/ha — for remarkable concentration in flavour and texture.

Why Vibrant

Pierre Berlancourt is one of the most humble and passionate winemakers, quietly pursuing his own vision without chasing trends. Despite tiny production and modest appellations, he consistently outperforms his classification.
